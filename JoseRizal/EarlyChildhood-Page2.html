Rizal's Early Childhood Life

He was the seventh child of Fancisco Rizal Mercado and Teodora Alonzo y Quintos. Jose grew up in a wealthy
family and was a child prodigy. He learned the alphabet by the age of 3. He could both read and write by the age
of 5.

1869

At the age of eight, Rizal wrote his first poem entitled “Sa Aking Mga
Kabata.” The poem was written in Tagalog and had for its theme “Love
of One’s Language.”

DECEMBER 17 1870

Having finished his studies in Binan Laguna, Rizal returned to Calamba
on board the motorboat Talim. His parents planned to transfer him to
Manila where he could continue his studies.
